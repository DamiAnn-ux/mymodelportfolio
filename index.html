about me

My name is Dami Ann — a fashion model who found passion in both the editorial/commercial and the web. My journey started in front of the camera, where I learned the art of expression, detail, and storytelling through poses and fabric. Modelling taught me discipline, creativity, and how to bring ideas to life visually. Over time, that same creative energy led me to the tech world, where I now build digital spaces that speak as boldly as I do in front of the lens. I’m currently exploring Frontend Development, merging fashion aesthetics with clean, modern web design. I believe beauty isn’t just what you see; it’s what you experience. Whether I’m styling a shoot or writing code, I’m always creating something that inspires.
